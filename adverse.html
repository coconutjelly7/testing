Health Problems


Health as defined by the World Health Organisation (WHO) is a state of complete physical, mental and social well-being and not merely the absence of disease or infirmity. Everyday we are bombarded with information and advertisements about healthy living as well products and ways which promote a healthy lifestyle.

While this helps us to live out healthy lives, we must also understand what factors can cause adverse effects to our well-being. This section intends to inform you about some very common detriments to your health and to help you make a better decision.


Below are 2 common practices which can negatively affect your health:

  • Smoking
  • Drinking

While there are many other factors that can also have adverse health effects, these 2 are the most common and preventable causes.
